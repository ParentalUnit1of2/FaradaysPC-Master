About Faraday's PC

  • August 11, 2022
  • Ghost
  • John Doe

Hello! If you are reading this, welcome to Faraday's PC I am learning about web design and selling all of our stuff (aka junk). I am working on putting up more items, as well as trying to redesign the website to be more mobile friendly. It is definitely a huge learning curve with all of this.

It seems that I learn something and I do it, then I realize that yes that is how you do it, yet it is better another way. Now I am in the process of compressing all my photos to make them load faster. This will take some time since I have almost 3,000 photos to do. At least I have figured this out before I got deeper into uploading photos. Thank goodness for jDownloader2!!

One other item that I am also working on is making a search function so that you can more easily find a certain item. This is taking me a bit of time since I am first and most importantly working on compressing all my images.

Please stick around for my journey into web design and selling everything for this move. I am very excited to get out of Kansas and get somewhere the heat isn't so horrible!
