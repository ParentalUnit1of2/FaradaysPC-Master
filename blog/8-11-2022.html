YouTube Challenges

Hello again from Faraday's PC!! Creating a website has been a challenge for me. I have never done anything like this before. Trying to learn everything from YouTube videos is quite the challenge since I have to find one that I can understand. I am a visual learner, so reading does nothing to help me, I must see it done to understand it. A lot of people speak faster than I can keep up, rewind seems to be my best friend. Yea, I know I can slow videos down, yet that seems to irritate me.

Hearing and Language Issues

The other thing for me is I have issues with my hearing, I can hear things, yet a lot of times I cannot understand what they are saying. This is an issue I deal with on a daily basis, yet is exaggerated when I am watching videos. I have to be careful if a person's first language isn't English since it is harder for me to follow along sometimes. This can be quite irritating to me since a lot of the good videos I have a hard time understanding. I have tried using the captioning, yet that isn't always correct.
