Turbulence and Where it all came from

Hello again from Faraday's PC!! Creating a website has been a challenge for me. I have never done anything like this before. Trying to learn everything from YouTube videos is quite the challenge since I have to find one that I can understand. I am a visual learner, so reading does nothing to help me, I must see it done to understand it. A lot of people speak faster than I can keep up, rewind seems to be my best friend. Yea, I know I can slow videos down, yet that seems to irritate me.

The other thing for me is I have issues with my hearing, I can hear things, yet a lot of times I cannot understand what they are saying. This is an issue I deal with on a daily basis, yet is exaggerated when I am watching videos. I have to be careful if a person's first language isn't English since it is harder for me to follow along sometimes. This can be quite irritating to me since a lot of the good videos I have a hard time understanding. I have tried using the captioning, yet that isn't always correct. The last thing I want to do is try and decipher what they are saying AND decipher the captioning. 😖

So a good majority of the items that we have for sale were given to us by my husbands parents. When we decided that we were moving to Seattle we needed to get rid of them. My mother in law collected bears, bears, and EVEN more bears! I have taken pictures of SO many bears that I may go insane if I find any more that I have to take pictures of. I can say, she was VERY dedicated to collecting them that is for sure. 🐻 🐼🧸

My father in law had his own collection of items. He collected tools, many of the tools I don't even think he knew what they were. As we try and price items Google Lens seems to be our best friend. Some of the items, even Google Lens has no idea what are either. Sometimes we may get lucky if we use Google Images. We still have so many tools that we have no idea what they are. Trying to price them have been harder. I gladly do the tools over the bears any day! At least for now....🔧🗜🔨⚒️🛠️🧰
